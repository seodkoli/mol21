Signs that a tree needs to be removedAs a tree owner, it's important to keep an eye on the health and condition of your trees. Trees provide numerous benefits such as shade, beauty, and oxygen production, but they can also become dangerous if they are diseased or damaged. Knowing when a tree needs to be removed is crucial for the safety of your property and anyone who may come into contact with the tree.

A leaning tree can indicate root damage or structural issues that could cause it to fall unexpectedly. If you notice that a tree on your property is leaning, it's best to have it evaluated by a professional arborist to determine if removal is necessary. Another sign that a tree needs to be removed is if it has large dead branches or significant canopy dieback. Dead branches can easily break off and fall, posing a risk to people and structures below.
